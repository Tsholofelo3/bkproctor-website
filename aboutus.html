About Collections by BK Proctor

Discover the story behind Botswana's most innovative fashion brand, blending urban culture with local artistry and craftsmanship.

Our Journey

Founded with a passion for bold self-expression, Collections by BK Proctor is more than a fashion label — it's a movement. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a brand that represents creativity, culture, and community pride in Botswana and beyond.

Our Mission

We aim to inspire confidence through distinctive apparel and footwear that celebrates local talent. Every collection is designed to empower individuals to embrace their uniqueness while staying rooted in Botswana’s rich heritage.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✅ Unique designs inspired by Botswana culture.
  • ✅ Collaborations with local artists & creators.
  • ✅ Premium quality products at affordable prices.
  • ✅ Strong commitment to community upliftment.
